Join Our Mission

We're looking for passionate individuals who want to invest their energy into work that truly matters.

What We Look For

We don't focus on credentials or pedigree. Instead, we ask three essential questions:

  • 1. What have you already built?

    Show us something you've created. We care more about what you've accomplished than where you went to school.

  • 2. What are you dreaming to build?

    Tell us about the breakthrough you want to create and the problem you're obsessed with solving.

  • 3. Why does this matter to you?

    Help us understand your motivation. The best innovations come from people who deeply care.

Our Commitment

We're building something that will take years to fully realize. We want people who think in decades, not quarters. If you want to change the world through biotechnology, we want to change it with you. We welcome applications from anyone who shares our mission, regardless of background or experience.
