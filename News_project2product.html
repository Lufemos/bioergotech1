The BioShot Competition

Within the framework of the Taranto Biotech Days, the BioShot competition proved to be much more than a simple showcase for innovative ideas: it was a concrete launchpad for biotech startups.

Organized to promote a culture of innovation and support Taranto's transition toward a center of biotechnological excellence, BioShot highlighted the talent of young entrepreneurs and researchers, rewarding solutions capable of addressing global challenges in health, environment, and sustainability.

During the first edition of BioShot in October 2024, nine startups had the opportunity to present their innovative projects before an audience of investors, experts, and institutional representatives.

The Winners

Four exceptional startups emerged from the competition, each demonstrating remarkable innovation and significant post-competition development.

1st Place

GenoGra

Applied Genomics Innovation

Innovation

GenoGra stood out for its innovative approach to applied genomics, offering high-precision genetic analysis platforms for the early diagnosis of rare and chronic diseases.

Post-Competition Success

After its victory, GenoGra initiated collaborations with companies such as BGenetica to validate its platform, participating in international acceleration programs.

Key Achievements

  • Partnership with BGenetica for platform validation
  • Integration in clinical genetics laboratories
  • International acceleration program participation
  • Enhanced credibility in biotech sector
2nd Place

Youth Health Tech

Universal Healthcare Prevention

Mission

Youth Health Tech distinguished itself with an ambitious vision: making prevention accessible to everyone, transforming prevention from a privilege into a universal right.

Technology

The startup develops digital solutions that enable anyone to monitor and optimize their health status in a simple, fast, and personalized way using AI and digital biomarkers.

Key Features

  • AI-powered screening and monitoring tools
  • No specific medical expertise required
  • Personalized prevention plans
  • Data-driven health recommendations
3rd Place

CompagOs

Intelligent Clinical Data Management

Solution

CompagOs presented a solution for the intelligent management of clinical data and the secure sharing of information among patients, doctors, and healthcare facilities.

Post-Competition Growth

Recognition at BioShot enabled the company to connect with technological and institutional partners, obtain local funding, and launch a beta version of the platform.

Achievements

  • Connected with technology partners
  • Secured local funding
  • Beta platform launch with local clinics
  • Gathered valuable user feedback
Special Mention

CranioTech

Cranial Disorders Diagnostic Innovation

Innovation

CranioTech, a startup from Taranto, received special recognition for developing an innovative patent for the diagnosis of cranial disorders.

Major Recognition

CranioTech earned several prestigious Awards and made technical progress for their proposal

Key Developments

  • Consolidated partnerships with research centers
  • Clinical validation phase advancement
  • National Awards winner
  • Established as regional medtech reference

The BioShot experience proved to be a springboard for all startups involved, fostering access to investors, strategic collaborations, and successful product launches.
