How We Support Entrepreneurs

Incubation & Spin-offs

Transform research breakthroughs into viable companies. Spin-off AI solutions developed through our healthcare partnerships. Access to lab facilities, technical infrastructure, and development resources. Guidance from ideation through product-market fit.

Funding & Investment

Seed funding for promising ventures. Access to our investor network across Europe, US, and Asia. Help securing follow-on funding from VCs and strategic partners. Equity positions that align our success with yours.

Mentorship & Expertise

Direct access to biotech industry veterans and successful entrepreneurs. Technical guidance from AI and biotechnology experts. Business development support and go-to-market strategies. Regulatory and compliance guidance for healthcare innovations.

Strategic Partnerships

Introductions to hospitals, pharmaceutical companies, and healthcare systems. Access to clinical validation opportunities through our network. Partnerships with institutions like Gilead Sciences, universities, and research centers. International expansion support across our global network.

Acceleration Programs

Fast-track promising technologies from lab to market. Access to existing patents and breakthrough research ready for commercialization. Technical validation and proof-of-concept development. Market analysis and competitive positioning.
